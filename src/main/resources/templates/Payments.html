Payments & Charges

My Car Is My Reflection PTY(LTD) is a company that is dedicated to keeping your vehicle(s) as neat as possible. The company is actually a 24/7 mobile car wash particularly made for people living around Durban. You can request a car wash at any point in time by filling in the form that way we can come over to clean any number of vehicles you want us to.
You can either choose a once off or a recurring deal with us. On the once off deal, you can request and then we come over and clean your vehicle(s) only once on the day and time of choice. On the recurring deal however, you can request and then we come over and clean your vehicle(s) every week day(s) and time(s) of choice.
In terms of charges and payments, we believe customer satisfactory is the number one thing. That is why, we start by doing the job first and then afterwards we charge the customer. However, we charge per vehicle. All private cars cost R150 each and trucks on the other hand have a different price, which is R200 each.
